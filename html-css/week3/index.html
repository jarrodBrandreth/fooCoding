How did it go then?

Where did the ancestors of the Moomins live? What was the wild youth of Moomin Papa like? What do the Hattivatis do during a thunderstorm? These and many other secrets will be revealed. exhibition.

In the main exhibition of the Moomin Museum, you can get to know the whole story of the Moomins, from the flood of the Great Flood to the floating theater of dangerous midsummer and the mystery of the lighthouse island to the mystery of the disappearance of the Moomin family in November.

Anniversary of the Moomins

2020 marks the 75th anniversary of the publication of the first Moomin book. In honor of the anniversary year, the Moomin Museum is participating in Moomin Characters' #MARKER SEA campaign to improve the situation in the Baltic Sea. The aim of the campaign, carried out in co-operation with the John Nurminen Foundation, is to raise awareness of the state of the Baltic Sea and to raise one million euros for the John Nurminen Foundation's work to protect the sea and its heritage. The Baltic Sea is our only sea, so take care of it!

Tampere House Shop

The Tampere House store has a high-quality selection of Moomin-themed products, Finnish design and handicraft products. The store serves both museum visitors and other guests of Tampere House. The selection includes exciting Moomin products, design and music, Moomin books written and illustrated by Tove Jansson, Tampere-themed products and Finnish classic brands. You can get stamps and Moomin Museum's own postage stamp for Moomin postcards.

Moomin Museum Reading Library

The Moomin Books written and illustrated by Tove Jansson fascinate both children and adults. In the library shelf, you can sit in peace and marvel at Tove's skill and use of the pen. There are treasures in the collection over the years and we are constantly getting new treasures in the library. The reading library attracts travelers in the main lobby of Tampere House and you can go there at any time when the house is open.
